Task Management for ADHD: The 3-Click Rule That Changes Everything

Meta Description: Learn the 3-click task management method that helps ADHD minds stay focused. Daily Dopamine Pro makes productivity simple, not overwhelming.

If you have ADHD, you've probably tried every task management system known to humanity. Notion? Too complex. Paper planners? Lost them. Sticky notes? They're currently forming a small civilization on your desk.

Enter the 3-Click Rule

Daily Dopamine Pro follows a simple principle: Any action should take 3 clicks or less. Why? Because ADHD brains lose focus in the gap between intention and action.

How It Works

  1. Click 1: Add task
  2. Click 2: Set time
  3. Click 3: Start

That's it. No categories, tags, priorities, or color-coding to obsess over.

Why Simple Beats Complex Every Time

  • Less setup = more doing
  • Fewer decisions = saved mental energy
  • Clear actions = reduced overwhelm
  • Instant start = momentum building

The Magic of Constraints

By limiting options, we're actually increasing your freedom to focus. You can:

  • Add custom background sounds later
  • Use YouTube or local files if needed
  • But you can also just... start

Remember: The best task management system is the one you actually use. Make it simple with Daily Dopamine Pro.
